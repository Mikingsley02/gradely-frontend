Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how gradely.info collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Gradely.info collects information necessary to provide school management services.

  • School Information: School name, school ID, admin ID, email address, phone number and subscription details.
  • Student Information: Student names, admission numbers, classes, attendance records, academic results and related educational records.
  • Parent Information: Parent names, email addresses and phone numbers where provided by the school.
  • Payment Information: Subscription plan details, transaction references and billing information processed through Paystack.
  • Technical Information: Login records, audit logs, IP addresses, browser information and security logs used to protect the platform.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information may be used to:

  • Provide access to the Gradely.info platform.
  • Authenticate school administrators, teachers, students and parents.
  • Generate and publish academic results.
  • Manage attendance, classes and school records.
  • Process subscription payments and renewals.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Improve platform performance and security.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Security

Gradely.info uses industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), password hashing, access controls, audit logging, authentication tokens, and secure third-party payment processing.

While we take reasonable steps to protect information, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted services:

  • Resend: To send emails securely
  • Paystack: For school subscription payments
  • Infobip: SMS notifications where enabled.
  • All third parties comply with data protection standards.

5. Subscription and Payment Information

Subscription payments are processed through Paystack. Gradely.info does not store full card details.

We may store subscription plans, transaction references, customer identifiers, billing status, renewal dates and related subscription information required to manage school accounts.

6. Data Retention

Student data is retained as long as the school remains active on our platform. Schools may request deletion at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion (via school admin)

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for parents and schools. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children under 13.

9. Policy Acceptance

By registering a school account, subscribing to a plan, or continuing to use Gradely.info, users acknowledge that they have read and accepted the applicable Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Refund Policy.

Gradely.info may record acceptance timestamps and policy versions for compliance and audit purposes.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use means acceptance of changes.

11. Legal Compliance

Gradely.info is designed to operate in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA), the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR), and other applicable laws governing privacy and data protection.
