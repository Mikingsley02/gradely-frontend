📚 Common Questions

How do I reset my password?
Go to the login page → Click "Forgot Password?" → Enter your Student ID or Admin ID → Follow the instructions sent to your email.
Where can I download results as PDF?
After logging in, navigate to your dashboard → Select the student or class → Click "Download PDF" to generate a printable report.
Can parents access results without internet?
No. Results are accessed through the Gradely portal when logged in. An active internet connection is required to view or download reports.
How do I upload student results?
Admins: Use the "Upload Results" tool in your dashboard. Download our Excel template, fill in the data, and upload the completed file. Ensure it's in .xlsx, .xls, or .csv format.

🔧 Troubleshooting

  • Login fails? Double-check your Student ID/Admin ID and password. Ensure Caps Lock is off and you're using the correct case.
  • No results showing? Confirm that data was uploaded to the correct academic term and year. Contact your school admin if you believe results are missing.
  • Page not loading? Clear your browser cache or try opening the site in incognito/private mode. Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
  • File upload failed? Make sure your file is in .xlsx, .xls, or .csv format and doesn't exceed 10MB. Check that all required columns are present in the template.
  • Forgot my Admin ID? Contact your school's system administrator or email support@gradely.info with your school name for assistance.
