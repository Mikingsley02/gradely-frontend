🍪 Cookie Policy

How we use cookies on Gradely.info

Last Updated: June 26, 2026

What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or use an online service. They help Gradely.info remember your preferences, keep your session secure, improve performance, and understand how the platform is used.
Types of cookies we use
Essential Cookies: These are required for secure login, authentication, fraud prevention, account protection, system functionality, and maintaining school, parent, and admin sessions. These cookies are necessary for Gradely.info to work properly and cannot be switched off through our cookie banner.

Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how users interact with Gradely.info, identify technical issues, improve page performance, and enhance the user experience. Where required by law, analytics cookies are used only with your consent.

Preference Cookies: These help us remember choices you make, such as cookie preferences, page settings, and other non-sensitive display preferences.

Optional or Marketing Cookies: These may be used to provide relevant updates, measure communication effectiveness, or improve user engagement. These cookies are not essential and will only be used where you have given consent, where consent is required.
How we use cookies
Gradely.info uses cookies and similar technologies to protect user accounts, maintain secure sessions, remember cookie choices, support core platform features, improve service performance, and understand how users navigate our website. We do not use cookies to sell personal data.
Lawful basis for cookie processing
Essential cookies are processed because they are necessary to provide a secure and functional platform. Analytics, preference, optional, and marketing cookies are processed based on consent where required under the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDP Act) 2023, the General Application and Implementation Directive (GAID) 2025, and applicable guidance issued by the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC).
Your choices
You may accept all cookies or reject non-essential cookies through the cookie banner where available. Essential cookies will remain active because they are required for login, security, and the proper operation of Gradely.info. You may withdraw or change your consent at any time by adjusting your cookie preferences or browser settings.
Managing cookies
You can manage cookies through your browser settings by deleting cookies, blocking cookies, or setting alerts when cookies are being used. You may also revisit the cookie consent banner, where available, to update your preferences. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect login, security, and core platform functionality.
Third-party cookies and service providers
Some trusted third-party services may use cookies or similar technologies when supporting Gradely.info, including hosting, payment processing, email delivery, SMS delivery, analytics, and security-related services. These service providers are expected to apply appropriate technical and organisational safeguards consistent with applicable Nigerian data protection requirements.
Cookies and personal data
Some cookies may collect or relate to personal data, such as IP addresses, device information, login activity, session identifiers, or usage logs. Where cookies involve personal data, Gradely.info handles that information in line with our Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and applicable Nigerian data protection laws.
Changes to this Cookie Policy
We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our technology, legal obligations, platform features, or regulatory guidance. Where changes are material, we may notify users through the website, dashboard, or other appropriate channels.
🇳🇬 Nigeria Data Protection Compliance
This Cookie Policy is designed to comply with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDP Act) 2023, the General Application and Implementation Directive (GAID) 2025 issued by the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC), and other applicable Nigerian privacy and cybersecurity laws.
Questions about Cookies or Privacy? If you have questions about our cookie practices, consent, or your rights under the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDP Act) 2023, please contact us at support@gradely.info.
